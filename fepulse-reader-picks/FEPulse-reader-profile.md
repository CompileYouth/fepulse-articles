# FEPulse Reader Profile

This is the default audience profile for FEPulse.

Use it after every scan to decide what should move from the broad archive into `fepulse-reader-picks/`.

## Core Audience

- programmers
- software engineers
- tech leads and engineering managers
- developers highly sensitive to how AI is changing programming

## What They Prefer

- programmer-focused interviews
- code-related interviews
- AI coding
- changes in development workflow
- how AI is rewriting software engineering
- how engineering teams use AI to raise output
- first-hand sharing from builders, engineers, and developer tool creators
- content with practical impact on career, engineering skill, or product judgment

## What They Usually Care Less About

- pure macro narratives
- investor or policy takes without engineering implications
- broad social commentary
- interviews with weak relevance to coding, building, or engineering
- abstract discussions without practical takeaways

## Selection Priority

High priority:

- AI coding
- engineering practice
- developer tools
- agent workflow
- product-engineering collaboration
- how teams use AI in real work

Medium priority:

- builder interviews
- founders discussing product and engineering organization
- technical product stories from 0 to 1

Low priority:

- macro industry commentary
- pure investing narratives
- social or cultural commentary

## Selection Questions

Ask these after every scan:

1. Is this directly relevant to programmers, code, or engineering practice?
2. Can this become a WeChat article that programmers would actually want to open?
3. Does it offer first-hand experience instead of generic opinion?
4. Could it change how readers work, build, choose tools, or judge their own skill path?

If the first two are weak, it usually should not enter `fepulse-reader-picks/`.
